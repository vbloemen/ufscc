=== ListDD Options

*--ldd-step*='STEP'::
        The internal tables of ListDD resize according to the Fibonacci series.
        This option sets the initial size to the Fibonacci number 'STEP'.
        Defaults to 30.

*--ldd-cache*='DIFF'::
        Set Fibonacci difference 'DIFF' between the cache and nodes (DIFF may be negative).
        Defaults to 1.
